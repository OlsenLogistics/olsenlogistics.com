We have extensive experience hauling all kinds of freight. With well maintained late model reefers, no matter what you need moved, we can get it done.

Our commodities

Produce

We move produce out of all major growing regions. We are also CARB certified, so we can legally operate in California. Proper load securement and precise temperature management are critical with produce, and we always ensure this is done the right way.

CARB certified

Meat

Our trailers are kept properly sanitized and maintained in food grade condition at all times. Cross contamination is not an option — every load of meat gets a clean, inspection-ready trailer.

Dairy

We haul a full range of dairy products kept at the temperatures your load requires. For milk loads that require a tanker endorsement, we have drivers qualified to handle them.

Frozen

Our late model reefer trailers are equipped to meet the intense demands of frozen freight, including ice cream. They are able to effortlessly maintain temperatures as low as -20°F.
